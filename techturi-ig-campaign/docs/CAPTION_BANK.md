## Caption structure (the formula)

Every caption follows this shape:

```
[HOOK — 1 line, specific, no clickbait]
[Blank line]
[BODY — 3–5 short lines, educational or narrative]
[Blank line]
[CTA — 1 line, where to go next]
```

Instagram truncates at ~125 characters. Front-load the hook.

## Caption rules to follow

1. **Hook first.** Instagram truncates at 125 characters. If your first line is "Hey everyone!" you've already lost.
2. **Short lines.** One idea per line. Generous whitespace.
3. **One CTA per post.** Don't make the reader choose.
4. **No emojis in the body.** Used sparingly in the hook if at all. The brand is editorial, not cheerful.
5. **No "follow for more"** language. The content should earn the follow.
6. **Link in bio, always.** Change the bio link to match the current post's CTA when it matters (weekly is fine).
