# Reel 21 — 11 Certifications. Ranked. (Day 21)

**Duration:** 30–40 seconds
**Format:** Vertical 9:16 (1080×1920)
**Recording method:** Screen-record the `reel-21-cert-ranking.html` animation
**Voiceover:** None

---

## Why this format

"Ranked" content is one of the highest-saving formats on Instagram. People screenshot tier lists. Every save is a distribution signal to the algorithm.

---

## How to produce

1. Open `reel-21-cert-ranking.html` in Chrome, fullscreen (F11)
2. Screen-record
3. Refresh the page to trigger animations
4. Let it run ~35 seconds
5. Stop recording
6. CapCut: crop to 9:16, add music, export at 1080×1920

---

## Content strategy

This Reel uses a classic **S/A/B/C tier** ranking format. It's bold, opinionated, and shareable.

Rankings:
- **S-tier:** OCI GenAI Professional, Google Data Analytics
- **A-tier:** PSM II, PSPO II, PL-300 (Power BI)
- **B-tier:** AWS Cloud Practitioner, ITIL Foundations
- **C-tier:** Generic "soft skill" certs, most vendor-specific ones you'll outgrow

The opinion builds credibility. People remember opinions; they forget neutral summaries.

## What NOT to do

- Don't rank anyone else's cert collection — only yours
- Don't soften the opinion ("some might say..."). The strong take is the whole point.
- Don't trash the C-tier certs publicly by name if they're from companies/vendors you have relationships with
