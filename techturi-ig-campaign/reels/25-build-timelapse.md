# Reel 25 — From Blank Page to Deployed (Day 25)

**Duration:** 40–50 seconds
**Format:** Vertical 9:16 (1080×1920)
**Recording method:** Typography-only animation (screen-record the companion HTML file)

---

## Why this format

This Reel pitches the **custom HTML/React** side of the studio — the premium offering. It needs to feel serious and considered, not flashy. Typography over stock footage is exactly the right tone for this audience.

---

## How to produce

The simplest version uses the same technique as Reel 11 and 21 — screen record an HTML animation.

For this specific Reel, optionally:
- Record a short segment of your code editor (VS Code) building a Next.js component (10–15 seconds)
- Blend it into the typography animation at the midpoint
- This shows "we actually code" without being distracting
