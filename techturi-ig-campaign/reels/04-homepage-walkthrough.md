# Reel 04 — Homepage Walkthrough (Day 4)

**Duration:** 30–45 seconds
**Format:** Vertical 9:16 (1080×1920)
**Recording method:** Screen recording of your live techturi.org site, edited in CapCut (free)
**Voiceover:** None — music + text overlays only

---

## Why this format

Screen recordings of real products convert. People have seen a thousand AI-generated stock videos; they haven't seen your actual site. The goal is: "this site looks intentional and real — who built it?"

## Music

**Free options** (no copyright strikes):
- Epidemic Sound (7-day free trial, cancel before charge) — search "minimal electronic"
- Artlist.io free library
- YouTube Audio Library — filter by "Cinematic" and "No attribution required"
- CapCut's built-in royalty-free library — look for lo-fi electronic or clean minimal beats

**Vibe:** Restrained. No big drops. Think a quiet tech brand ad (Linear, Stripe, Vercel style), not a hype reel.

---

## Text overlay style

In CapCut:
- **Font:** JetBrains Mono if available; otherwise any clean monospace
- **Color:** `#F5F4EE` (bone) with accent words in `#D4FF3A` (chartreuse)
- **Size:** Readable on mobile — minimum 60pt
- **Animation:** Simple fade in + out. No sliding, no bouncing.
- **Position:** Alternate top and bottom thirds to avoid covering Instagram's UI

---

## Recording tips

1. **Use Chrome incognito** so your bookmarks bar doesn't appear
2. **Hide the URL bar** by pressing F11 for fullscreen (then ESC when done)
3. **Macbook:** QuickTime Player → File → New Screen Recording → Selection only
4. **Windows:** Xbox Game Bar (Win + G) → record
5. **Scroll slowly and smoothly** — trackpad two-finger with light pressure works better than mouse wheel
6. **Record at 60fps** if your laptop can handle it; smoother final output
7. **Record longer than you need** — it's easier to cut than re-record
